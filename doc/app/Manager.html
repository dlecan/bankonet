Manager

Bankonet on Rails. Copyright © 2011 SQLI
Author: Philippe Gu√©gan (pguegan@sqli.com)

A manager of Bankonet. To access the Bankonet Administration application, a manager will be requested to sign in by providing his username and password. However, managers are not allowed to access Bankonet standard application, as it is reserved to Bankonet customers.

Every manager is able to create, edit or delete any customers profiles and accounts descriptions. He is also allowed to transfer money between accounts from different customers, or create any operation such as debitting or crediting a customer account.

A manager cannot create or access other managers profiles.
